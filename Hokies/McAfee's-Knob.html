What to Expect

Located in Roanoke Virginia, McAfee’s Knob is a MODERATE DIFFICULTY hiking trail that leads to a beautiful scenic view on top of Catawba Mountain. Similar to The Cascades, McAfee’s Knob has a fire trail. If you are new to hiking I would recommend taking the fire trail to the overlook because the trail is quite strenuous and narrow with lots of rogs, logs, and stairs. Be advised the top of the mountain is cooler and windy, no matter what time of year, so dress in layers.Parking spots are limited; However, street parking is allowed at your own risk because the entry way to the trail is right off the highway filled with speedy cars.
