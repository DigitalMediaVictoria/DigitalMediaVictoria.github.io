What to Expect

Located in Pembroke,Va,The Cascades is an EASY LEVEL hike alongside a ravine that leads to a beautiful waterfall. The total distance start to finish is approximately 4 miles; 2 miles to the waterfall and 2 miles back to the parking lot. The hike is a little rocky with a decent amount of stairs, both carved into the landscape and made of rocks. However, there is a fire trail for an even easier hike to the waterfall. Using the fire trail will cut your hiking time in half. There are no trashcans along the trail so please be courteous and collect your trash to throw away at the end of your hike. Swimming is allowed at the waterfall but at your own risk.

Be advised that parking is $3with each visit.
